The Story

RedesCreation started as a small sketchbook and a love for character-driven narratives. Over the years it evolved into a full studio producing cartoons, comics, and animated shorts for brands and storytellers across the globe. Our approach emphasizes shape language, color storytelling, and emotional resonance.

We work with authors, indie studios, and commercial brands to develop characters that stand the test of time — both playful and memorable. From initial thumbnail sketches to final vector-ready assets, our pipeline is optimized for both creativity and clarity.

FAQ

Common questions about process, revisions and licensing.

How long does a project take? ▾
Small projects: 1-2 weeks. Illustration series or animation: 3-8 weeks, depending on scope.
What is your revision policy? ▾
Starter includes 1 revision, Pro includes 2-3. Studio work offers custom revision terms.
Do you provide source files? ▾
Yes — PSD, layered files or vector formats available on request depending on the package.
Can you animate existing characters? ▾
We can rig and animate pre-existing assets or offer redesigns for animation-friendly splits.

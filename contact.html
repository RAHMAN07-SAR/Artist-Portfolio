Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a workshop?
Workshops are listed on the events page — use the contact form to request private sessions.
Can I license artworks?
Yes, licensing enquiries can be sent to licensing@redescreation.in (or via the form).
Are prints available?
Selected prints are available in our shop. Contact us for out-of-print pieces.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes — shipping rates vary by country. See shop checkout for options.
How long does a commission take?
Timelines vary by scope. Small pieces may take 2-4 weeks; larger projects longer. Discuss via email.
Can I collaborate with the studio?
We welcome collaborations — include details and your portfolio in your message.

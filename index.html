About RedesCreation

FAQ

Answers to common questions about process, licensing and delivery.

How long does a project take?▾
Turnaround depends on scope — small characters: 3-7 days, full campaigns: 2-6 weeks. We'll provide timelines in the project brief.
Do you provide commercial licenses?▾
Yes. Licensing options are included in the Pro package and can be added to Studio packages.
Can you animate my existing characters?▾
Absolutely — we can rig and animate existing assets, or rework them for animation-friendly splits.
Do you accept international clients?▾
Yes — most projects are handled remotely via a straightforward workflow and online reviews.
What tools do you use?▾
We use common industry tools including Procreate, Photoshop, Illustrator and After Effects. We deliver final files in PNG, PSD, SVG or MP4 as required.
